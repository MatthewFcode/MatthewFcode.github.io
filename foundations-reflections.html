Human Skills Overview

NEW SKILLS AND LEARNING- I am not sure how much more I have learnt about myself other than the fact that some of my self-talk tendencies seem to follow a negative pattern. However my ego seems to remain despite this which is confusing.

EMPATHY AND SELF AWARENESS- I think empathy towards yourself can be quite important when it comes to programming. However I believe the biggest empathy you can have for yourself is staying on top of your work load so you can feel relatively confident in your work ethic and enable yourself the opportunity for growth by putting in the effort.

BIGGEST SUPRISE-What has surprised me most is how transferable the skills are towards the programming side of the course and how mindset and habit are all key factors in achieving the programming success.

BIGGEST CHALLENGE- The most challenging aspect was getting myself and my mind on board with accepting the focus on them and putting in effort to think about and reflect on them.

DEV ACADEMY ON HS-I think the reason that devacademy is spending so much time focusing on human skills is so that the students don’t come out as one dimensional people who can just code. They want the students to come out as the full package of people with technical skills but also the social skills to succeed in the work environment socially and also in the habits they create for themselves in the future.

TIME SPENT ON HS-I don’t necessarily think that the time I have spent studying human skills has necessarily been a waste of time, however I do think that I had built up knowledge prior to the course and had a decent level of self awareness when it comes to these sorts of topics but learning more about them and reflecting has been super cool.
